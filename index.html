Physicist · Quantum Materials

Amarnath Chakraborty

Ph.D. Physics · University of Missouri–Columbia  ✦  Quantum Research Intern · Chinar Quantum AI

I grew up in Jhargram, West Bengal and trained through Midnapore College, the University of Hyderabad, and JNCASR Bangalore before completing my Ph.D. at UMC under Prof. Giovanni Vignale. My work spans emergent quantum phenomena in 2D materials — band topology, magneto-optical response, and quantum geometry — now converging toward hybrid quantum–classical simulation workflows. Outside physics, I'm an avid reader who believes the best ideas live at the edges of disciplines.

Research Vision

What I'm Building

⚛️ Quantum Computing
Quantum Hamiltonian Simulation ▾
Classically compressed SKQD on IBM heavy-hex hardware, targeting correlated phases of the Fermi-Hubbard model.
Quantum Reservoir Computing ▾
Quantum dynamics as a natural reservoir for computation — I'm exploring this as my entry point into quantum ML.
🔬 Quantum Materials
Twistronics ▾
Moiré engineering in twisted bilayer graphene — correlated flat-band physics.
Altermagnetism ▾
Altermagnetic responses via quantum geometric tensor signatures in 2D materials.
🎯 Long-Term Goal
Generative Hybrid Quantum–Classical Workflow
A modular pipeline coupling classical simulation, quantum simulation, and ML — where physics-based solvers generate training data for correlated systems, feeding interpretable predictive models that accelerate quantum materials discovery.
