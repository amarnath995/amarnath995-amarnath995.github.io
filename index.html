I grew up in Jhargram, West Bengal and studied physics through Midnapore College, the University of Hyderabad, and JNCASR Bangalore before completing my Ph.D. at Missouri under Prof. Giovanni Vignale. My work spans emergent quantum phenomena in 2D materials — band topology, magneto-optical response, and quantum geometry — and is now converging toward hybrid quantum–classical simulation. Outside physics, I'm an avid reader who believes the best ideas live at edges of disciplines.
